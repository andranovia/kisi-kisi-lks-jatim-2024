Instruction

Objective:

Obtain the most points on the hexagonal board during the game

Gameplay:
  • Players take turns selecting hexagons on the board.
  • Red and blue players alternate turns.
  • Place hexagons strategically to take over opponent's hexagons.
  • Adjacent hexagons of the same color increase in value by 1.
  • Game ends when no empty hexagons remain.
